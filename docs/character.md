# Character
Represents the character a persona plays within the game.
Characters have attributes, abilities and equipment.
For now a Persona has a Character (1 to 1 relation).

## Leveling
Characters can level when reaching certain experience (xp) threasholds.
Leveling means increasing base attributes and unlocking training points (tp).

## Training Points
The player can increase the effectiveness of a Characters ability or learn new abilities using training points.

## Character Professions (Specialization)
Characters can specialize in certain professions.
(Persona involvement, how would certain persona enjoy a certain profession)

Starting professions - Mystic, Fighter
Advanced Mystic specializations:
 - Cleric/Paladin?
 - Magician
 - Warlock
 - Bard

Advanced Fighter specializations:
 - Warrior
 - Ranger/Hunter
 - Tarikat
 - Bard
 - Paladin

## Character ability
Characters have an active and a passive ability.
Character starts off with an active one and unlocks a passive when specializing (choosing a profession)
