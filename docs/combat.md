# Combat

## Board
Characters are split in 2 opposing group, 4 characters per group
Each group has 2 front-line and 2 back-line positions.

Target order is as follows position 1 front-line > 2 front-line > 1 back-line > 2 back-line

## Combat Performance
Performance is a function of the group cohesion (relations.md), member know-how (know-how.md) and character strength (equipment + level).

## Cohesion:
Bonuses
 - less or more tension per battle
 - more know-how per battle
 - more chance of "lock-in event"

 - less damage taken (more glancing, less critical)
 - more critical % less glancing


"Lock-in" happens when you have high cohesion, group buff (think bloodlust).
Lock-in increases casting/attack speed.
Other bonuses? Like extra trust and such?

Breakpoints:
 - forming - 20% of LI per battle
 - storming - 5% of LI per battle
 - norming - 30% - 40% of LI per battle
 - performing - 80% of LI per battle
