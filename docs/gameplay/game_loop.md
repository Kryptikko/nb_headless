# Game Loop
Each turn is represented with a day, so the Player has a finite amount of action points.
Possible action are:
  - Recruit
  - Assign Activities
    - Assemble Parties for Multiplayer Activities
    - Single Player Activities
  - Handle Rewards from Activities
  - Manage Guild Resources
    - Craft (or assign some 1 to craft)
    - Assign (read. Equip) Items to Members
    - Trade
  - Progress (through Activities?)
    - Guild Levels
    - Member Levels (give direction aka, pick specialization)
  - Handle Internal Relations
  - Handle Events

# Progression Mechanics
Progression is handled by short and long term goals with soft and hard gates.
- Guild Levels
- Member Levels and Specializations
- Member Equipment Progression

## Long Term Goals
  - Content Progression - Clear all the content

## Short Term Goals
  - Guild Levels [Guild.md], unlock certain mechanics. Guild Levels serve as hard gates and are a way to slowly introduce the Player to the mechanics without overwhelming him.
    - Guild Experience is gained as Reward for clearing a new dungeon for the first time
    - Reaching Guild goals (example: recruit 5 members, clear 1 dungeon, specialize a member)
  - Crafting Recipes - Crafting recipes are essentially a todo list, they server as both a trigger for the player to explore content (finding certain materials), with small power boosts.
  - Member Character Level progression
  - Member Character Equipment progression
  - Member Persona SYMLOG alignment
