
# Character Specialization
After Player Characters reach a level threshold, they are have the option to specialize their character.
1st specialization is lvl 6, second at lvl 12 (since the level cap is 20 this will allow for the Player too play around with a fully enabled character from 12 to 20)
This transition is descided by the Player represeting the role of a mentor pusning pupils into specific role and the need of an organization to have more specilized members as it grows for further efficiency. 

# Specialization process
When Exploring the over-world there are specific points of interest that enable certain speciazation processes.
Examples:
School of Dark Arts - enables the warlock specilization quest-line

Specialization quest lines can be tied to crafting specific equipment or the character that needs the specialization will require to clear a certain activity (Boss Encounters Activties for the purpose of completing the spec)
A good idea is to require certification from multiple PoI to achieve the final spec, that way the player has room to optimize the process.

# Specialization power boost
Specialization enables new spells for that character, and disables some of not all of the existing ones.
Each specialization has a attribute template (values for STR,INT,CON) that provide different gear stats scaling.
