# Guild Members
Guild Members or just Members are Personas and their Character that are part of our guild.

# Recruitment
Recruitment is a Player Action, it can be a result of interview/trail or event.

## Recruitment Interviews and other Humiliation Rituals
A Recruit is a Personas and their Character, that are not yet part of the guild.
Recruitment Interview is a Player Action where the Player trades action points to gain details on a recruit.

The Player has limited visibility on recruit details.
### Recruit Details:
- Character Profession & Specialization - Free
- Equipment and Stats - Cost
- Persona Relationship Archetype (in case of IPA model) - Cost 
- Persona Needs Archetype (in case of needs based AI) - Cost 

TODO: expand the list?
- Traits - Cost

### Process
1. Inspect the Recruitment Board - a list of available recruits and the current information you have for them
2. Interview process - The Player is presented with a chat window and active conversation options, each of which reveal parts of the Recruit Details.
3. The Player decide whether they are going to accept the recruit or end the process. - Any Action points spend during the interview are lost.
4. Each question has a small chance that the recruit loses interest and stops the process. - Action points spend during the interview are still lost.
